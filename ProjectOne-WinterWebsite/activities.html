My favorite activites
Inside Outside
My favorite inside activity to do in the winter is baking. From brownies to banana bread, Playing music and baking can be very calming and eating my creations while watching Netflix feels like heaven. My favorite outside activity to do in the winter is taking picture of nature after a snow storm. After the roads are cleared and it is save to drive on the road, driving while listening to music can be relaxing. Minnesota is a very beautiful state in the winter.
